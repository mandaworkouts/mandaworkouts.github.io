Ordering rules

  • At least one easy/rest day between the three hard sessions (intervals, tempo, bike)
  • Never stack intervals and tempo back to back
  • No intervals the morning after a heavy lower-body day — tempo or easy sits better there

Guardrails

  • 🦵 Left hip/glute: activation work before every speed session
  • 🌡️ Above ~18°C: add 5–8s/km and judge by effort/HR
  • ⏭️ Missed session? Just move on — never cram two hard days together
